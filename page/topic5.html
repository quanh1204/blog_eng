My Shopping Habits: Balance Between Wants and Needs

  • I try to balance my shopping habits between what I want and what I need. I am not a shopaholic, but I enjoy shopping sometimes. I usually shop for clothes and household appliances at local stores because I like to see the items before buying them. For electronic devices and flight tickets, I prefer shopping online. I shop online about once a month, mostly for items like books, electronics, and sometimes clothes. My favorite website to shop online is Amazon because it has many products, and I can read customer reviews before buying. I usually pay with my credit card because it is easy and safe.


    Shopping online is convenient because I can shop from home, there are many products, and I can compare prices easily. However, there are some disadvantages, like not being able to see or touch the product before buying it and sometimes receiving items that do not match the description. I like to buy sale-off items because I can save money, but I always check the quality and reviews first. I have had a few bad experiences, like receiving damaged goods, but they do not happen often. I do not often bargain when I shop because most places have fixed prices.


    When shopping online, I consider price, customer reviews, and product origin. After-sale services are also important to me, especially for electronic devices. In conclusion, I enjoy shopping both online and in-store, and I always try to make smart choices based on my needs and preferences.
